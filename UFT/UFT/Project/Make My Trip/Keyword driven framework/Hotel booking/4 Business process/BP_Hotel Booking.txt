Business Process Of Bus Booking:
BP01: Launch Internet Explore.
BP02: Open MakeMyTrip as http://www.makemytrip.com/
BP03: Click on Hotel Link
BP04: Click On Domestic Link
BP05: Fill all Sections Like Destination, CheckIn Date, CheckOut Date, Adult, Children 
BP06: Click Search Hotel Link
BP07: Click First Book Now Link
BP08: Select First Book Now Link (as selection of room) 
BP09: Fill All Sections of Traveller Details as FirstName, LastName, Age, EmailId, MobileNo.
BP10: Click On Check Box as i am booking for myself
BP11: Click On Continue To Payment Link
BP12: Close Application
