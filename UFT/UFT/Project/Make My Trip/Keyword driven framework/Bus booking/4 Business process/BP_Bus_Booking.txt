Business Process Of Bus Booking:
BP01: Launch Internet Explore.
BP02: Open MakeMyTrip as http://www.makemytrip.com/
BP03: Click on Bus Link
BP04: Fill all Sections Like From,To,Departure Date and Email as Optional
BP05: Click Search Buses Link
BP06: Click First Select Bus Link
BP07: Select Seat 
BP08: Click On Continue Link
BP09: Fill All Sections of Traveller Details as FirstName, LastName, Age, EmailId, MobileNo.
BP10: Click On Continue To Payment Link
BP11: Close Application
